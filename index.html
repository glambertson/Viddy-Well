Viddy Well

Viddy Well is a jQuery Plugin that makes working with Video JS even easier. If you're not familiar, Video JS is an open source HTML5 video player. Additinally, Video JS allows you to serve up a single .MP4 file for all browsers and provides a fallback Flash-based player for the oldies that don't support HTML5 video.

Download

What's The Point?

HTML5 video is a requirement if you want to view video on tablets and phones. Video JS makes that possible through a refined, open source framework. That said, I found Video JS easy to implement but also found that others (non developers) didn't find it as easy. So my hopes are that Viddy Well can help non developers share in my enthusiasm for Video JS.

Mobile Too

Would you want to load a full-size video on your phone via your data connection? Me neither. Prep a mobile version of your video in .MP4 (H.264) format, a mobile poster image, set the VideoHasMobile to true and Viddy Well will deliver those mobile assets to your mobile audience.
